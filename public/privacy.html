Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TPSmart handles your information when using QR Scanner Free, including Google Sign-In, Google Cloud cloud synchronization, advertising services, and local device features.

Camera & Gallery Permissions

QR Scanner Free requires access to your device camera and image gallery to scan, capture, import, and decode QR codes and barcodes. All scanning and image processing are performed locally on your device. We do not upload, stream, or store your camera feed or gallery images on our servers.

Authentication & Account Services

Users are not required to create an account to scan QR codes. Google Sign-In is optional and is only used for cloud synchronization and account-related features.

You may optionally sign in using your Google account through Google Cloud Authentication.

When you sign in, we may receive:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL (if available)
  • Google Cloud User ID (UID)

This information is used solely to authenticate your account, associate your cloud data, and enable synchronization features. We do not receive or store your Google account password.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use QR Scanner Free, we may collect or process:

  • Google account information (email address, display name, profile photo)
  • Google Cloud User ID (UID)
  • Scan history, favorites, and settings when synchronization is enabled
  • Device identifiers used by advertising providers
  • Application usage and diagnostic information

This information is used to provide authentication, synchronization, application functionality, security, analytics, customer support, and advertising services.

Cloud Synchronization (Google Cloud)

If you sign in and enable synchronization, your scan history, favorites, settings, and supported application data may be securely stored in Google Cloud.

Cloud synchronization allows you to access and restore your data across devices using the same authenticated account.

Data is protected using Google Cloud Security Rules, encryption in transit, and Google's infrastructure security controls.

Local & Cloud Data Storage

Your scan history is stored locally on your device by default.

When synchronization is enabled, a copy of eligible data may also be stored securely in Google Cloud.

You may delete local history at any time through the application settings.

Advertising & Analytics

To keep the application free, we display advertisements using Google AdMob and mediation partners. These providers may collect information such as Advertising ID, device information, app interaction metrics, and approximate location derived from network information.

Such information is processed according to the privacy policies of the respective advertising providers.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Account & Data Deletion

Users may delete their account directly within the application through the Account Settings screen.

For additional assistance, users may also submit a deletion request through our account deletion page:

https://tpsmart.co/delete-account.html

When an account deletion request is completed, account-related information and cloud-synchronized data associated with the user will be permanently removed within a reasonable period unless retention is required by law, security obligations, or fraud prevention purposes.

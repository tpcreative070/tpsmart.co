Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TPSmart handles your data within the QR Scanner Free mobile application and our associated landing pages. We are fully committed to protecting your mobile privacy.

Camera & Gallery Permissions

Our app requires access to your device's camera and local image gallery to look for, capture, and decode barcode or QR code images. All image processing is executed entirely on your local device. We do not upload, stream, store, or transmit your camera feed or image files to external servers.

Third-Party Advertising (Disclosures)

To keep this utility free, our application displays third-party advertisements via Google AdMob networks. These external ad service providers may access and collect basic device processing identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) to serve relevant, personalized ads and track performance metrics in accordance with their own respective privacy rules.

Local Device History Logs

Your historical code scans and CSV exports are stored safely inside your device's internal application sandbox. You retain total authority over this data and can delete your local history records at any time through the in-app settings panel.
